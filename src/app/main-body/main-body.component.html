SYMPTOMS OF CORONAVIRUS

Fever

Fever is a key symptom, experts say. Don't fixate on a number, but know it's really not a fever until temperature reaches at least 100 degrees

Cough

Coughing is another key symptom, but it's not just any cough, said Schaffner. It should be a dry cough that you feel in your chest.

Difficulty Breathing

Shortness of breath can be a third -- and very serious -- manifestation of Covid-19, and it can occur on its own, without a cough

DONATE BLOOD AND SAVE LIVES

Many people are seeking for Plasma Donation to save their lives, so who are recovered from COVID-19 can donate their blood and help people

  • You can donate blood for corona patients, if you have recovered from covid-19 and completed the 14 days quarantine and tested corona negative.
  • You must seek doctor advice about your blood group and your eligibility to donate blood.
  • You must have at least 18 years old a weight of 50 kgs to donate your blood.
  • You cannot donate blood if you are suffering with any other blood related health issues.
  • Donating blood also improves your cardiovascular health and reduces obesity.

PREVENTION OF CORONAVIRUS

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Avoid touching

Avoid touching your eyes, nose or mouth,especially with unwashed hands

Avoid close

Avoid close contact with people who are sick.

Cover your mouth

Cover your mouth and nose with your arm or tissues to reduce the spread of germs.

Clean and disinfect

Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces.

Stay home

Stay home if you are sick to avoid spreading illness to others.
